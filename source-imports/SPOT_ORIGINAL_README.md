# Spot!

Spot! is a protection tool focused on grooming recognition, predatory targeting, coercion, manipulation, and abuse-risk around children and youth.

The name comes from a simple instinct: spot the warning signs early, spot what others may miss, and help the right person act sooner. A small dog sidekick fits the product naturally because it suggests watchfulness, loyalty, and early warning without making the tool feel cold or clinical.

Its purpose is to help identify early warning signs, preserve evidence, explain danger in plain language, and route the user toward the safest next step as quickly as possible.

## Core Promise

- spot manipulative or predatory patterns early
- protect first, diagnose second, respond last
- help a parent, guardian, witness, or older youth understand what may be happening
- support documentation and escalation
- connect region-based rights and reporting guidance when criminal behavior may be involved

## Core Modes

- `Who are you?`
- `I am the parent or guardian`
- `I am a witness`
- `I am the young person who needs help`
- `Something feels wrong`
- `Could this be grooming?`
- `What should I do right now?`

## Product Direction

- safety first
- plain-language explanation
- parent-guided use for younger children
- youth-aware routing for older youth
- fast escalation when risk is high
- support for witnesses and protectors, not only direct targets
